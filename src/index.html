FAQ

How does MAYAK WORKSHOP approach the process of creating 3D visualizations for architectural projects?

At MAYAK WORKSHOP, we begin by thoroughly understanding the architectural design, its objectives, and its unique features. Our team then utilizes a combination of cutting-edge software and artistic expertise to meticulously craft 3D visualizations that accurately convey the design's essence, from structural details to lighting and textures.

How does your team manage collaborative projects when your specialists are located in different countries?

Despite our geographical dispersion, our collaboration is seamless thanks to modern communication tools. Regular virtual meetings, shared project management platforms, and efficient communication channels enable us to work together effectively, leveraging each team member's strengths to deliver exceptional results.

What level of customization can clients expect when working with MAYAK WORKSHOP?

We understand that every project is unique, so we tailor our approach to meet individual client needs. Whether it's specific materials, lighting conditions, or design elements, we prioritize customization to ensure our visualizations align with the client's vision and project goals.
